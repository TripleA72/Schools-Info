WELCOME TO CRESCENT MODEL HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL

The journey of Crescent Model School started in 1968 with a small group of leading educationists with big dreams of providing affordable education to the young generations of Pakistan. A handful of students has now turned into a student body of 7600, one of the largest not for profit schools in Pakistan which is no less than a miracle. With a rich history exceeding fifty years, the Crescent Model Higher Secondary School has maintained its glorious tradition of providing quality education, trained staff and merit based and need blind admission policy. We strive to provide quality education for the next generations and enlighten our students intellectually, morally and spiritually during their years with us. And that is our vision for 2050.
